Frequently
Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about our dental care, treatments, and visits so you know what to expect.

How often should I visit the dentist?+

We recommend a routine check-up and cleaning every 6 months for most patients.

What can I expect during a dental procedure?+

Our team walks you through every step, ensuring you stay comfortable and informed throughout.

Do you offer emergency dental services?+

Yes — we offer same-day emergency appointments whenever possible. Call us right away.

What should I do if I have a toothache?+

Rinse with warm water, avoid very hot or cold foods, and contact us to book an urgent visit.

Do you treat children?+

Absolutely. We see patients of all ages and offer a gentle, kid-friendly experience.

How can I keep my teeth and gums healthy?+

Brush twice daily, floss every day, eat a balanced diet, and visit us for regular cleanings.
