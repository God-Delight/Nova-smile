FAQ

Need Help?

Answers.

Still curious? Our friendly team is always happy to chat.

How much does teeth whitening cost?+
Pricing depends on the treatment plan we recommend for you. Contact us directly and we'll give you a clear breakdown with no hidden charges.
Is the procedure painful? +
Most of our treatments are completely painless. We use gentle techniques and where necessary, local anaesthesia to make sure you're comfortable throughout.
How long does a dental procedure take?+
It depends on the treatment. A routine cleaning takes about 30 to 45 minutes, while procedures like veneers or implants are spread across a few appointments.
Can I get veneers if my teeth are stained or uneven?+
Yes, veneers are actually one of the best solutions for stained, chipped, or uneven teeth. Book a consultation and we'll assess what works best for your smile.
Do I need to book an appointment or can I just walk in?+
We recommend booking ahead to avoid waiting, but we also welcome walk-ins. Call us or send a message and we'll fit you in as soon as possible.
